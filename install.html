Add to Home Screen

For the best experience, add this slideshow to your home screen. It will open full-screen without any browser tabs or address bars.

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Open this site in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (square with an upward arrow) at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the upper right
  5. Open it from your home screen

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open this site in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu in the upper right
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  4. Tap "Add" to confirm
  5. Open it from your home screen
